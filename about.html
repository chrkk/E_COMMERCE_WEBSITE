Our Story

Once upon a time, in a bustling city filled with tech enthusiasts and entrepreneurs, two friends, Armando and Ermilita, shared a passion for all things computers. They spent countless hours tinkering with hardware, building custom PCs, and exploring the endless possibilities of technology. Their journey began in a small garage, where they would gather after school to work on their latest projects. With a shared vision of creating a space where fellow tech enthusiasts could come together, learn, and collaborate, they decided to turn their hobby into something more. Armed with determination and a deep love for technology, Alex and Emily pooled their savings and set out to establish their own PC building business. They scoured online forums, attended tech conferences, and networked with industry professionals to learn everything they could about the art of PC building. With their combined expertise and unwavering commitment to quality, Alex and Emily's reputation quickly spread throughout the community. Customers were drawn to their attention to detail, personalized service, and genuine passion for their craft..
